Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. This application operates with complete transparency:

  • No internet connection required or used
  • No data leaves your computer
  • No user tracking or analytics
  • No persistent storage of your files
  • Files processed in memory only
  • Automatic cleanup on exit

In simple terms: This app works exactly like a desktop calculator - it helps you do something useful, then forgets everything.

Why This Project Exists

This tool was created specifically for criminal defense attorneys who receive discovery in massive PDF files containing dozens of different documents - police reports, witness statements, lab results, photos - all bundled together. It's a pain to work with, and I needed an easy way to split them apart.

The real problem: Most PDF tools require uploading files to "some company's" servers. That's unacceptable when dealing with client data. I don't want my clients' sensitive information sitting on someone else's computer, being processed by who knows what, stored who knows where.

So I built this tool with these principles:

  • Complete privacy - I know exactly what's happening with my client data (nothing leaves my computer)
  • Reliability over features - Built with stable, proven libraries instead of the newest trends
  • Built to last - Avoids dependencies that might break next year
  • Just works - A tool I can trust and depend on for years to come

This wasn't built to be the most feature-rich PDF tool. It was built to do one thing well: split PDFs privately and reliably, so criminal defense attorneys can process discovery without compromising client confidentiality.
